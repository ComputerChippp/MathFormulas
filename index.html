Formulas

Check out the formulas I offer.

  • Area of a Rectangle: \( A = l \times w \)
  • Perimeter of a Rectangle: \( P = 2l + 2w \)
  • Area of a Triangle: \( A = \frac{1}{2} \times b \times h \)
  • Area of a Circle: \( A = \pi r^2 \)
  • Circumference of a Circle: \( C = 2 \pi r \)
  • Pythagorean Theorem: \( a^2 + b^2 = c^2 \)
  • Volume of a Rectangular Prism: \( V = l \times w \times h \)
  • Simple Interest: \( I = P \times r \times t \)
  • Slope of a Line: \( m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \)
  • Quadratic Formula: \( x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
  • Mean: \( \text{Mean} = \frac{\text{Sum of all values}}{\text{Number of values}} \)
  • Median: Arrange values in order, then select the middle value (or the average of the two middle values).
  • Probability: \( P(E) = \frac{\text{Number of favorable outcomes}}{\text{Total number of outcomes}} \)
  • Expansion of a Binomial Square: \( (a + b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2 \)
  • Expansion of a Binomial Square 2: \( (a - b)^2 = a^2 - 2ab + b^2 \)
  • Expansion of a Binomial Square 3: \( (a + b) (a - b) = a^2 - b^2 \)
